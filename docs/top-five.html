Aine's Top 5 Videos

I really enjoy these videos!

  1. "Champagne Coast" - Blood Orange. A song I consider perfect, and its accompanying music video is super cool.
  2. Harry Potter Puppet Pals - The Mysterious Ticking Noise. This video would make my friends, siblings, and I laugh so hard when we were little. It was watched over and over again.
  3. The Cinnamon Challenge ... by Glozell and her big behind earrings. Just an early YouTube classic that most people my age have probably stumbled upon at some point.
  4. Tik Tok - Ke$ha Parody. Another early YouTube classic but this was specifically a hit with my siblings and I. My older brother is who introduced me to it which made it extra cool and funny.
  5. Lana Del Rey - "Ride". The only music video ever.
